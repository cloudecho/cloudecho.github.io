Privacy Policy for "Joy 2048"

Effective Date: March 2, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At "Joy 2048", we are deeply committed to your privacy and believe in 
transparency. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data.  
However, in this case, the most important practice to understand is that 
"Joy 2048" does not collect any personal data from you.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We want to be absolutely clear: "Joy 2048" is designed and built to 
function without collecting any personal data from its users.

This means we do not collect information such as:

Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, 
IP address, usernames, or account names.

Demographic Data: Age, gender, location, interests, or preferences.

Usage Data: Information about how you use "Joy 2048", such as pages visited, 
features used, time spent, or interactions within the service.

Device Information: Type of device, operating system, unique device identifiers.
Location Data: Precise or approximate location information.

Cookies or Tracking Technologies: We do not employ cookies, trackers, web
beacons, or similar technologies to collect user data.

2. Why We Don't Collect Data

Design Philosophy: "Joy 2048" is intentionally designed to operate locally 
on your device or within your browser without needing to transmit or store 
personal data.

Functionality: The core functionality of "Joy 2048" does not require us to 
collect or process any user data to operate effectively.

Privacy as a Priority: We believe in minimizing data collection as a core 
principle to protect user privacy.

3. Children's Privacy

"Joy 2048" is committed to protecting children's privacy.  Because we do not 
collect any personal data, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) 
and similar regulations do not apply to "Joy 2048" in the context of data 
collection.

4. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any 
significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. We 
encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.  
The "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy indicates when it was 
last revised.
